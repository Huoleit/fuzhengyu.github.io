Quadruped Walking Mechanism

A mechanical system consisting of four identical planar walking mechanisms and being propelled by friction with the ground.

Video

Mechanical Design

(source: Jansen's linkage)

I used Theo Jansen's Leg Mechanism for this walking machine. Legs are grouped for both sides and are driven by motors respectively. For each group, legs have the same rotation speed but differ in phase. A ball wheel is mounted at the back to form a stable triangular shape.
